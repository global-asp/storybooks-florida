Veja os animais Look at the animals Mira a los animales

Jenny Katz

Sandy Campbell

Priscilla Freitas de Oliveira

Alfredo Ferreira

Portuguese

Level 1

Veja os animais.

Look at the animals.

Mira a los animales.

A vaca diz, “muuu.”

The cow says, “Moo.”

La vaca dice, “Muu.”

A cabra diz, “mé, mé.”

The goat says, “Meh, meh.”

La cabra dice, “Be, be.”

O cavalo diz. “iihhh.”

The horse says, “Neigh.”

El caballo dice, “Hin.”

O porco diz, “ronc, ronc.”

The pig says, “Grunt.”

El cerdo dice, “Oink-oink.”

A galinha diz, “cocoricó.”

The chicken says, “Cluck.”

La gallina dice, “Cló-cló.”

O cachorro diz, “au, au.”

The dog says, “Woof.”

El perro dice, “Guau.”

O fazendeiro diz, “xiu!”

The farmer says, “Shhh!”

El granjero dice, “¡Chsss!”

Written by: Jenny Katz
Illustrated by: Sandy Campbell
Translated by: Priscilla Freitas de Oliveira
Read by: Alfredo Ferreira
Language: Portuguese
Level: Level 1
