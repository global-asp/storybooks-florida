Languages

The following languages are currently included in the scope of the Storybooks Florida project:

  • English
  • Spanish
  • Haitian Creole
  • Antillean Creole / Dominican Creole
  • French
  • Portuguese
  • German
  • Tagalog
  • Vietnamese
  • Italian
  • Arabic
  • Chinese
  • Russian
  • Ukrainian
  • Polish
  • Lao
We are always looking for translators! If you speak one of the above languages and would like to help us translate stories, please contact us at globalafricanstorybook [at] gmail [dot] com
